PROBLEM STATEMENT & SCOPE
-----------------------------------
The Problem: Worker Safety and Regulatory Compliance is Under Attack in Heavy Industries.

Challenges in the Ecosystem:
1. Manual safety monitoring is reactive, leading to delayed responses to accidents.
2. Lack of real-time visibility into high-risk zones (e.g., gas leaks, temperature spikes).
3. Permit violations (e.g., Hot Work in hazardous areas) are difficult to track synchronously.
4. Compliance audits (Factory Act, OISD, DGMS) are manual, slow, and prone to human error.

Scope of Innovation:
We are building a real-time Safety AI Platform that:
1. Uses Computer Vision and IoT Telemetry to detect safety violations (PPE, gas levels).
2. Leverages LLMs to auto-generate corrective actions and safety briefings.
3. Provides a live Safety Dashboard and Audit Agent for instant hazard triage and compliance tracking.


WORKING BACKWARDS FROM THE CUSTOMER
-----------------------------------
Primary Customer:
Plant Safety Operations & Compliance Officers

Need: Early detection of compound risks (e.g., Gas + Hot Work) and automated compliance auditing.
Pain Point: Current rule-based alarm systems are isolated, have high false positives, and lack contextual intelligence.

End Users Benefiting:
- Workers: Protected from hazardous environments and sudden accidents.
- Shift Managers: Receive clean, prioritized, and auto-resolved alerts without manual triage.
- Auditors: Live regulatory coverage monitoring without manual paperwork.

Why It Matters:
Worker safety is core to industrial operations.
Faster hazard detection = reduced injuries, improved compliance, and zero downtime.

SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
-----------------------------------
End-to-End Architecture for Real-Time Safety

Flow Overview:
Telemetry Ingestion -> Ingests live IoT and CV data from camera streams and sensors.
Golden Path Engine -> State machine monitoring permit conflicts against environment.
Orchestrator Engine -> Evaluates risk scores and triggers escalations/compound risks.
LLM-based Action Agent -> Generates corrective actions and regulatory reports via RAG.
Safety Dashboard -> Real-time React/Next.js interface for triage & alerts.

Tech Stack:
AI/LLMs: OpenAI, LangChain, OpenCV
Backend: Python, FastAPI
Frontend: Next.js, React, Framer Motion, Tailwind CSS


DETECTION LOGIC AND CORE DATA FLOW
-----------------------------------
How the Detection Pipeline Works:
Sensor Telemetry (CH4, H2S, Temp): Flags critical thresholds and LEL breaches.
CV Analysis: Detects PPE violations (e.g., missing vests, unauthorized entry).
Golden Path State Machine: Monitors permit conflicts (e.g., Pipeline Welding in elevated CH4).
Alert Generation: Safety AI Dashboard receives categorized events and triggers evacuations in real time.


DEEPER INSIGHTS: AUDITS, PERMITS, INCIDENTS
-----------------------------------
Quality & Compliance Audit Agent:
Live coverage against OISD, Factory Act, DGMS. Uses LLM-powered analysis to assess compliance status and dispatches automated corrective actions (e.g., SAP PM work orders) when deviations occur.

Permit Monitor:
Tracks active permits vs environmental hazards. Visualizes semantic drift in safety conditions over time, immediately revoking permits like Hot Work if compound risks are detected.

RAG Pattern Discovery:
Discovers root causes from incident logs. Surfaces patterns in safety behavior across time and zones, enabling Trust & Safety teams to investigate systemic protocol loopholes.
